SymbolicIntegration.jl (my GSoC project)

Author: Mattia Micheletta Merlin; Date: 20/07/2025

Google summer of code is a summer program in which students around the workd get paid to contribute to open source projects. Summer 2025 I am partecipating, building a symbolic integrator (a program that does indefinite integrals, finds primitives) for the Julia language. (github repo) This program works by applying integration rules on the integrand based on it's form. I didn't write the rules myself but I am taking them from a Mathematica package called Rubi.
